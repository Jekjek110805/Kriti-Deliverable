Frequently asked questions

Is MAAI just ChatGPT for SEO?
No. MAAI is grounded in your actual Search Console data and follows a defined scoring model and SOP. Generic chat tools guess without your data; MAAI reasons about real performance and produces prioritized, defensible recommendations.
Does MAAI replace Google Search Console?
No — it sits on top of it. Search Console reports what happened; MAAI is the decision layer that tells you what to do about it, then routes work through approval and back to monitoring.
How does MAAI decide what to optimize?
Each keyword is scored on a 100-point model across position, impressions, search intent, commercial potential, and whether a matching page already exists — then ranked by priority with a clear recommendation and reason.
Do humans stay in control?
Always. Nothing is published automatically. Ordered approval gates, role-based access, and a full audit log keep a human in the loop at every stage.
What data does MAAI need?
A Google Search Console connection (live) or a CSV/XLSX export containing query, impressions, clicks, and position. That's enough to start scoring opportunities.
Can MAAI create the content too?
Yes. Once you approve an opportunity, MAAI generates an intent-aware brief and a full AI draft, then runs SEO, fact, and brand checks before it reaches your approval queue.
